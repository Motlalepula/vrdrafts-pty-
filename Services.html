Services

From humble beginnings, VR DRAFTS has grown in to multi-layered construction company. We pride ourselves in providing our clients with a residential and commercial turn key property management and construction solution. Equipped with years of experience and professionals in Architectural Services, Supplying of material, Project Management, Site Managment and Quantity Surveying as well as a professional team of Builders and Engineers. This complete approach to Architectural Services and Construction has led to VR DRAFTS providing out clientele with the most affordable services and quality assured.

What VR DRAFTS can do for you:

VR DRAFTS Construction and Trades, specializes in providing our clients with a unique solution for all your construction, maintenance and architectural structure needs. We have a highly experienced team with our employees specializing in all duties of construction or maintenance. No job is ever to big or small.

The services we offer:

  • Renovations/building construction
  • Plumbing
  • Electrical engineering and drafting
  • Tiling
  • Architectural Plans
  • Interior design
  • Carpentry (Built-in Cupboards/Kitchen Unit)
  • Rib and blocks manufacture and supply
  • Maxi and stock brick manufacture and supply

By providing our clients with our unique services, we can guarantee all work by our members, as well as provide our clients with an affordable price.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

  • We understand fully the importance of producing exceptional quality products and the fore we have put in place measures to ensure that all our products and services offered are of high quality Our client service department ensures that briefs are taken correctly and with attention to detail, that the client is able to see work in progress at various stages and that all work is signed off before application or installation
  • Top Management provides evidence of its commitment to the development, implementation and improvement of our QMS in very tangible ways:
  • Our quality policy statement (QMS) documents and communicates the importance of meeting or exceeding all applicable requirements (including customer, regulatory and legal requirements) through continual improvement of our processes, products, and services.
  • We ensure that our quality policy is understood, implemented, and maintained at all levels of the organization through widespread printed distribution of our quality policy statement, and through periodic management review of the quality policy statement and corporate level improvement objectives (QMS). In addition, our quality policy and objectives are communicated and deployed throughout the organization through individual performance objectives established and reviewed during employee performance reviews-KPI’s (QMS).
  • All managers demonstrate their commitment to the development and improvement of the QMS through the provision of necessary resources, through their involvement in the internal audit process (QMS), and through their proactive involvement in our continual improvement activities (QMS) – where emphasis is placed on improving both effectiveness and efficiency of our key QMS processes
